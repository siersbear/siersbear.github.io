Allergy Disclaimer

At MysticBites, we take allergies and dietary restrictions seriously. However, due to the nature of our menu which includes dishes containing mystery ingredients or undisclosed components, we are unable to guarantee that any dish is completely free from allergens or other dietary concerns. Our chefs use a variety of ingredients and techniques, some of which may not be disclosed in our menu descriptions. While we strive to accommodate special requests and provide accurate information about our dishes, we cannot guarantee that cross-contact with allergens will not occur.

We encourage guests with food allergies or dietary restrictions to inform our staff before placing their order. Our team will do their best to accommodate your needs and provide guidance on menu options. However, please be aware that consuming food at Mystic Bites is done at your own risk, and we cannot assume liability for any allergic reactions or adverse effects that may occur.

If you have any questions or concerns about allergens or ingredients, please don't hesitate to ask a member of our team for assistance. Your safety and satisfaction are important to us, and we appreciate your understanding. Thank you for choosing MysticBites. We look forward to serving you soon.

Have any other questions? Feel free to leave us an email or check out our FAQ!
