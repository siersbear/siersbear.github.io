MysticBites Terms & Conditions

Reservation Policy:

  • Reservations are highly recommended to ensure availability, especially during peak hours.
  • We reserve the right to cancel or modify reservations based on availability and operational needs.
  • For large group reservations or special events, please contact us in advance to make arrangements.

Dining Experience:

  • We strive to provide an exceptional dining experience to all our guests. Please notify us in advance of any special requirements or dietary restrictions.
  • While we make every effort to accommodate special requests, we cannot guarantee the availability of all ingredients or dishes.

Menu and Pricing:

  • Our menu offerings and prices are subject to change without prior notice.
  • All prices are listed in the local currency and are inclusive of applicable taxes and service charges.
  • Additional charges may apply for extra services such as corkage fees or special requests.

Payment Terms:

  • Payment is due at the time of service unless prior arrangements have been made.
  • We accept cash, credit/debit cards, and other forms of electronic payment. Personal checks are not accepted.
  • For group reservations or catering services, a deposit may be required to secure the booking.

Cancellation and No-show Policy:

  • If you need to cancel or modify your reservation, please notify us at least 24 hours in advance to avoid any cancellation fees.
  • In the event of a no-show or late cancellation, a fee may be charged to cover the cost of the reserved table and lost revenue.

Health and Safety:

  • The health and safety of our guests and staff are our top priority. We adhere to strict hygiene and sanitation protocols in accordance with local regulations.
  • Guests are required to comply with any health and safety guidelines implemented by the restaurant, including wearing masks when not seated and practicing social distancing.

Feedback and Complaints:

  • We value your feedback and strive to continuously improve our services. Please feel free to share your comments, suggestions, or concerns with us.
  • If you have any complaints or issues during your visit, please bring them to the attention of our staff immediately so that we can address them promptly.

Intellectual Property:

  • All content, including but not limited to logos, images, and menu descriptions, are the intellectual property of MysticBites and may not be used without prior written consent.

Privacy Policy:

  • We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. Any data collected during your visit will be used solely for the purpose of providing and improving our services.
  • We do not share your information with third parties unless required by law or with your explicit consent.

Refund Policy:

  • Notify us immediately if you're unsatisfied; we'll address it promptly.
  • Gift card purchases are non-refundable but can be used indefinitely.
  • Refunds typically process within 5-7 business days.
  • Full refunds are available for canceled reservations with at least 24 hours' notice.

Miscellaneous:

  • These terms and conditions constitute the entire agreement between you and Mystic Bites regarding your visit to our restaurant.
  • We reserve the right to refuse service to anyone for any reason.
  • By dining at MysticBites, you agree to abide by these terms and conditions.

Thank you for choosing MysticBites. We look forward to serving you and creating unforgettable dining experiences together!
